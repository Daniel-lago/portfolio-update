Dev-Connect

This is the last proyect I did in my bootcamp, we create a platform for Programmers freelancers and Recruiters to connect each other. In Dev-connect programmers will be able to create a profile and add their skills in programming, also they can put their highlights proyects, experience and basic information about them. In the other side, recruiters will be able to look for candidates and check their profiles, filtering by skills, experience, knowledge and contact with them. Dev-connect was developed with a full MERN stack, using RESTful API, Apollo Server and Apollo Client to get everything working.
